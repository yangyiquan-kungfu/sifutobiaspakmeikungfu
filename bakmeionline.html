The Art

Power Born From
Precision

Bak Mei is not a performance art. It is a highly explosive internal system built on short-distance strikes, refined body mechanics, and ferocious conditioning. Using low kicks, defensive structure, and devastating limb-destruction methods, it grants the ability to generate power from any angle or range.

The core syllabus — 5 hand sets, 4 weapon sets, and 3 meditations — is deliberately compact. Rather than overwhelming you with dozens of forms, every movement is studied until it becomes instinct. The meditations refine your intent, your energy, and your internal spirit.

"Bak Mei is not about flashy forms — it is about building spirit, precision, and the ability to end a fight with decisive force."

— Sifu Dane Tobias

Your Teacher

Sifu Dane
Tobias

Founder · Bak Mei Online · Wollongong, Australia

Sifu Dane's journey began with his father — an avid Judoka who trained him at home, instilling discipline and movement from childhood. In his teens, he found himself in rough environments, using martial arts for real-world self-defense and direction in equal measure.

He later built a career as a highly regarded chef, working alongside culinary legends like Rick Stein and Jamie Oliver. But the intense pressure of Michelin-star kitchens took its toll. That's when Shifu Angelo Clori introduced him to the Cao Tung System — learning Southern White Crane, Mi Zong Chaan, and Shaolin Temple Boxing. Kung Fu became his salvation.

When Dane encountered Bak Mei, he felt immediately at home. His background in real-world fighting and deep understanding of body mechanics allowed him to recognize something rare — a system of extraordinary power and internal refinement. He committed fully.

Today, Sifu Dane wakes at 4am daily to meditate and train for two hours before heading to his Michelin-star kitchen. Alongside his practice, he studies Traditional Chinese Medicine under the Wong Fei Hung lineage.
