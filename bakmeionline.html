Your Teacher
Sifu Dane
Tobias
Founder · Bak Mei Online · Wollongong, Australia

Sifu Dane's path began with his father — an avid Judoka who trained him from childhood, instilling deep discipline and movement. In his teens, he found himself in rough environments, frequently defending himself in real street confrontations — martial arts became survival, not sport.

He later built a celebrated career as a Michelin-star head chef, working alongside legends like Rick Stein and Jamie Oliver. But the pressure of elite kitchens took its toll. That's when Shifu Angelo Clori invited him to learn the Cao Tung System — Southern White Crane, Mi Zong Chaan, and Shaolin Temple Boxing. Kung Fu became his salvation.

When Dane first encountered Bak Mei, he immediately recognized something extraordinary — a rare internal system of devastating precision and raw power. His background in real-world combat gave him instant understanding of what others take years to grasp. He committed fully and dedicated himself to mastering its depth.

Today, Sifu Dane wakes at 4am daily: meditate, train two hours, check in with students, prepare his children for school, then lead his Michelin-star kitchen. Alongside his martial practice, he studies Traditional Chinese Medicine under the Wong Fei Hung lineage and prepares authentic Dit Dar Jow liniments according to ancient Hakka herbal formulas.
